Frequently Asked Questions

How does ResumeHub AI protect my personal data?
Your resume and personal details are parsed locally or sent to our secure backend depending on your configuration. If you configure a self-hosted Gemini API key in Advanced Settings, all processing stays strictly local, sending data directly to Google's API endpoint rather than our servers.
Will the tailored resume look formatted?
Yes, when you click generate, we export your tailored experiences into clean DOCX, PDF, and TXT structures so they compile beautifully on MS Word, Google Docs, or PDF viewers.
Does this work on all job portals?
The integrated sidebar, salary estimator, and matching score overlay have custom support for LinkedIn, Naukri, and Instahyre. The automatic form auto-filler is designed to identify input fields across most standard job application platforms globally.
